### The philosophies start at home

"Put People on the Map" isn't only for clients. Every person on this crew gets a lane, a roadmap, and a public byline on the work — visible, heard, chosen, starting with each other.

### How the crew sharpens the blade together

<div class="grid2">
<div class="box"><strong>Paid field training</strong> — Crew earn while they learn the craft on real sites. Nobody trades free labor for exposure here.</div>
<div class="box"><strong>Trades with a "done"</strong> — Skill-for-skill trades are timeboxed and written down. Respect is a document, not a vibe.</div>
</div>
<div class="grid2">
<div class="box"><strong>The Monday loop</strong> — Monday sets the board (owner · deliverable · due) → all-week thread → Friday wins and stars. Honest reflection, every single week.</div>
<div class="box"><strong>Earned expansion</strong> — Roles grow when results land. The pilot defines the rate; the rate respects the person.</div>
</div>

<div class="stat">Try, fail, learn, improve — together, out loud, every Friday.</div>
