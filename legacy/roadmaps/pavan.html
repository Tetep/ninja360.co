The Ninja-360 Tech Build,
Staged & Sequenced

Tim Petet × Pavan Gantha (AZ Team). Seven milestones, all pointed at one target: the September push — fresh customers at scale. Each stage is scoped, has a "done means done" test, and gets paid as it lands — structure chosen per milestone: trade-out · milestone payment on completion · per-close fee.

Prepared 2026-07-13 · V06 (M6 belt test + ninja360.co locked in as the dedicated outreach domain: web 301 → ninja360.net, mail-only cold sends, August warm-up) · Source: Ninja-360 Master Hub open loops (2026-07-12 pass) + Audit Engine SOP v1.0 · Every item below is existing documented scope, not new invention

01 · How This Works

  • Pointed at fresh customers. Every paid stage builds acquisition — outbound, audits, pipeline, closes. Existing-client delivery (LiveSmart and the like) stays in-house with Tim and is not part of this agreement or its payments.
  • Sequenced, not lump-sum. The build is real and it's wanted — it gets done in stages, paid as each stage completes and verifies. No stage starts until the prior one passes its "done when" test.
  • Payments follow collected revenue. Milestone payments are funded from money that has actually landed; the LiveSmart stage pays from the LiveSmart go-live money itself.
  • Sizing set per stage. Pavan itemizes hours per milestone → Tim sets the number per stage before it starts. Blanks below are filled together, in writing.
  • The showcase: this system is the rails under a filmed, public 90-day campaign ("On the M.A.P."). The GHL build gets named credit on camera — a live portfolio piece for AZ Team in the KC market.

M5 · The War Chest — Prospect DatabaseBuild starts now · sends only after M1

Scope

  • Source waterfall (per the Ninja-360 prospecting plan): ACA roster → Gladstone Chamber → Liberty Chamber → Northland business clubs → Google (compliant tooling only). Beachhead verticals: restaurants → chiro → dental/ortho → Airbnb.
  • Every record: business name, vertical, city/zip, contact name, email, phone, website, GBP URL, source, consent status. Deduped + email-verified before it touches the CRM.
  • Loaded into GHL with tags + smart lists by location × vertical, segment-pull tested — campaign-ready.
  • Compliance, non-negotiable: NO SMS to scraped contacts (consent-only channel — standing hold applies) · cold email is CAN-SPAM clean (real address, working opt-out, honest subject lines) · sending architecture protects ninja360.net's domain reputation — Pavan proposes the setup before the first send.
Done when: each batch of 250 verified records lands deduped, tagged, <3% bounce on verification, and a test segment (e.g., "restaurants · Gladstone") pulls correctly in GHL. First target: 500 records.
Unblocks: the email + guerrilla + social campaign — the fresh-customer machine gets its ammunition. (Message/creative comes from the content lane; this milestone is data + rails.)
Structure: milestone payment per verified 250-record batch — $ per batch. Volume scales only as batches stay clean.
