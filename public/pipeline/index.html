The Nine Stages

0

Pick the business

Weekly feature target — an audit prospect, a route-adjacent business, or a high-traffic map listing.
Owner: Tim✓ Ready
1

Plan the route

Pre-production checklist: scope, airspace/LAANC, weather, gear check, client timing.
Owner: Tim → the System (SOP v3)✓ Ready
2

Shoot

Walk-through → exterior drone → interior 360s → stills → FPV. Fixed settings, in and out fast.
Owner: Tim (solo) · Gabe (premium tier)✓ Ready
3

Upload — at the car

Back up source, push to Cloud, upload to Google same day. Latency = lost moat views.
Owner: Tim → semi-automated◐ Needs build — 2-min "shoot wrap" form
4

Edit

Template, color grade, brand assets, tagging. Mechanics field-tested; the creative layer is Gabe's to design.
Owner: Gabe · Creative Director◐ Rates being finalized
5

Review & grade — at home

Tim reviews mocked posts, grades tagging/color/content in one batch. Nothing publishes without approval.
Owner: Tim — approval gate✓ Ready
6

Schedule & autopost

Approved assets onto the GHL Social Planner calendar; autopost runs on schedule.
Owner: Pavan · GHL◐ Needs build — pre-flight pending
7

Blog publish

"Inside [Business]" post on ninja360.net — embedded Google Map / 360 tour, story, audit CTA.
Owner: the site build◐ Needs build — blog template + per-business pages
8

Measure

Weekly cadence grade · monthly Maps view delta (moat growth) · audit-funnel entries from content CTAs.
Owner: the System — weekly cycle◐ Needs build — tracking wiring
The one bottleneck: Stage 4 · Edit. Locking Gabe's Creative Director rates (see the Game Plan's pay/split) unblocks the color-grade preset, the workflow design, and whether editing is Gabe-solo or Gabe-plus-a-volume-editor. Everything downstream is waiting on it — highest-leverage next move.
