  • B · Auto color — applies the Ninja LUT per camera
  • C · Auto nadir — stamps the Nadir preset on 360 + drone-down
  • D · Shedit — auto-cuts a V01 from the shot list + story template → drops in 03_EXPORTS
Pavan revives + extends your existing "Hasan Pipeline Builder" Apps Script — seed already exists.
