Three Doors

Same vision. Never pitch the whole system. Hand each person the one door that's theirs — the thing they care about most. Lead with their door; settle the terms after they're through it.

GabeCreative Director / Lead Editor
"You're not my editor. You're the Creative Director of a show that's also your reel."
What's in it for you
  • The title + the authority — you design the look: the grade, the templates, the whole edit craft.
  • Real paid creative work — client shoots and the show, on camera and in the cut.
  • Your work seen every week — the show is your portfolio and your audience.
  • Your own business (Lucid) hosted on our system; upside on deals your work helps close.
The ask: come design and run the creative. We settle your terms Monday (Gate 0) — this opens the door, it doesn't close the deal.
Open with"I want you as Creative Director — the show is your reel, and you design how we make all of it."
PavanSystems & Automation
"You build the machine that runs this — and I help you build and sell yours."
What's in it for you
  • You architect the machine — Ninja Warp Speed + the GHL builds. Real systems work.
  • Your own platform, in parallel — I help build it, and I coach your sales.
  • Live builds to point to — ours, Lucid, and Lucky 13 (Partner Hosting).
  • Your review engine can ride inside our belts once the cost's on paper.
The ask: keep building with me — 90-day sprints, a clear "done" on each. Your review-engine cost in writing first.
Open with"You build the machine — and I build and sell yours right alongside it."
ErikAllied Studio · Lucky 13
"Let's build a show in your studio — and I'll get Lucky 13 found in Gladstone."
What's in it for you
  • A co-host chair on a show with a growing audience + cross-promotion.
  • Your studio booked and in the credits.
  • I fix your visibility — your name on your own site, your Google right, found in Gladstone.
  • Your site + CRM hosted on our platform.
The ask: trade studio time for hosting + your visibility setup. Run 90 days and see how it feels. (Show lane — a partner, not inside the build.)
Open with"Let's make a show in your room — I'll get Lucky 13 found, you bring the studio and the mic, we both grow."
Remember: these open doors — they're invitations, not closed deals. Gabe's terms settle at Gate 0; Pavan and Erik are bounded 90-day trades with a clear "done." Lead with their door, let them get excited about what they get, then settle the terms. You're the guide handing each of them a way to level up — not someone asking for favors.
