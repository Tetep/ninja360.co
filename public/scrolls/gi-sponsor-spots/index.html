House Sponsor Spots

The first episodes carry sponsor reads for our own three companies — practice for real reads, promotion for the alliance, on-air proof the ad format works. One 60-second read + one 30-second cutdown each. Highlighted brackets are the only things left to fill before recording.

The two rules: Nobody reads their own company — "he's too modest to say it, so I will" lands warmer and rehearses how we'll cross-promote real sponsors. And we disclose: "full disclosure, this one's ours" on the first episodes builds the trust a paid read spends later. Drop it once outside sponsors arrive.

Delivery Notes

Rotate the reader. The owner never reads their own spot. End on one clean CTA + URL — that repetition is what makes a read stick. 60s is mid-roll, 30s is pre/post-roll. Read it like you're telling a friend about something you own — because you do.
