02 · Current State & Reconciliation

Live today: the Dojo runs at dojo.ninja360.co (private). The ninja360.co apex 301-redirects to ninja360.net and doubles as the dedicated cold-send domain for outbound (its own SPF/DKIM/DMARC, warmed through August) — so cold campaigns never touch the ninja360.net reputation.
  • Blog home migrates to blog.ninja360.net with 301s so existing SEO + the embed-don't-copy mechanism stay intact.
  • Email subdomains (noreply. + mail.) reinforce the same deliverability work flagged in the engine build (M1).
  • ninja360.com — acquire later when the price is reasonable; no immediate SEO benefit over .net today.
Why it works: matches the real brand identity (Ninja360 everywhere), kills the hyphen problem, scales SaaS + media delivery, boosts SEO through structured subdomains, protects the root reputation, and future-proofs for HighLevel + AI automation.
