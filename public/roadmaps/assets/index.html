An asset is anything that would still be valuable if you weren't around. This roadmap takes every finding and every tip from the Dent heatmap and turns it into the company-wide build order — executed in-house, layered onto the machinery already running.

01 · The Two Verdicts That Matter

Verdict 1 — Business Design: Lifestyle Boutique

Dent's classification: build a fun team of 3–12 technicians with high revenue per person, driven by a Key Person of Influence supported by smart digital assets, optimized for income, flexibility, and fun — and deliberately never more than 12 people. This validates the entire current structure: Tim as the KPI face, Draft Picks (Gabe, Pavan, Brent) instead of employees, digital assets instead of headcount. You are not under-building a big agency — you are correctly building a boutique. Stop apologizing for being small; the report says small-by-design wins.

Verdict 2 — Stress Is an Asset Deficiency

The report's diagnostic rule: anything causing stress or a bottleneck is a missing asset. No leads → Marketing & Sales systems deficiency (your reddest square). Everything running through Tim → Management & Admin deficiency. Nobody amplifying the brand → Ambassadors deficiency. This turns every complaint into a build task — the same move the visibility audit does for clients.
