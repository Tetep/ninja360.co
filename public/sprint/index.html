§2 The Weekly Rhythm — Non-Negotiable

Two fixed touchpoints per week. Everything else flexes around IDS field days; these two don't.

Every Monday · In person or video · NON-NEGOTIABLE

Monday Sync — Set the Week

  1. Punch list review: walk every open item — done, stuck, or carried. Add new items surfaced since Friday.
  2. Lane check: each lane states its ONE deliverable for the week (from §3 below).
  3. Logs review: time log totals, open issues, proposed solutions — decide on each solution: adopt, test, or park.
  4. Schedule: lay the week's work around confirmed IDS field days and shoot dates.

30–45 min. Miss it only for a client emergency — and it gets rescheduled within 24 hours, never skipped.

Every Friday · Async or quick call

Friday Follow-Up — Close the Week

  1. Existing projects: status ping every open engagement — WOs pending, AP confirmations, client waits (Josh/LiveSmart-style threads don't go stale).
  2. Invoice batch: run the Friday engine — every acceptance this week has an invoice queued or sent.
  3. Logs closed out: time entries complete for the week; new issues written down with at least one possible solution attached.
  4. Punch list staged for Monday — anything unfinished gets carried forward explicitly, not silently.

20 min. Friday is the leak-catcher — it exists because of Safelite and Wichita.

The Three Trackers

One shared tracker (sheet or GHL), three tabs. Rule: if it isn't logged, it didn't happen.
